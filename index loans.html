About Marongrong Cash Loans

Marongrong Cash Loan is a black-owned financial services company started by Johannes Pheshele Mashigo who wanted to start a business dedicated to transforming the South African Market by offering speedy short-term loans to help South Africans with their financial needs.

Our company was founded to empower South African citizens to gain access to affordable short-term and urgent loans. We believe in providing financial solutions that are accessible, transparent, and tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Marongrong Cash Loan offers short-term urgent loans to help relieve cash flow problems for clients in need of immediate cash assistance. With our simple application process and quick approvals, we're here to help you navigate through financial challenges.

Our Vision

To empower individuals with fast, accessible, and responsible short-term financial support that improves their lives and builds financial confidence.

We aim to bridge financial gaps, foster economic growth, and improve lives through responsible lending practices and exceptional customer service.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a loan if I am blacklisted?

Yes! Marongrong Cash Loans specializes in providing loans to individuals with poor credit history or who are blacklisted. We believe everyone deserves a second chance and focus on your current financial situation rather than past credit issues.

What documents do I need to apply?

To apply for a loan, you'll need to provide:

  • Valid South African ID
  • Latest 3 months bank statements
  • Proof of income (payslip or income statement)
  • Proof of address (not older than 3 months)
How long does the application process take?

Our application process is designed to be quick and efficient. In most cases, we can process your application within 24 hours. Once approved, funds are typically transferred to your account on the same day.

What are your interest rates?

Our interest rates start at 5% per month, which is competitive in the short-term loan market. The exact rate may vary based on the loan amount and term. We charge simple interest only, with no hidden fees or compounding charges.

What happens if I can't make a repayment?

If you're having difficulty making a repayment, contact us immediately. We offer flexible repayment options and can restructure your loan to help you manage your payments. We never charge penalty fees without discussing alternatives with you first.
